        What this covers
        • FAR 52.204‑21 (FCI basic safeguarding) and mapping to CMMC 2.0 Level 1.
        • DFARS 252.204‑7012/‑7019/‑7020/‑7021 (for CUI) and alignment to CMMC Level 2; Level 3 noted for select programs.
        • FedRAMP Moderate expectation when CUI is stored/processed by a cloud service provider.
        • ITAR/EAR data handling constraints (U.S. Person, export controls, cloud restrictions).
        • DHS overlays (DHS 4300A/B), FISMA/NIST RMF for systems operated on behalf of an agency.
        • Third‑party/flow‑down obligations and continuous monitoring basics.
        Accuracy & review
        • This navigator reflects widely published requirements: FAR 52.204‑21, DFARS 252.204‑7012/‑7019/‑7020/‑7021, CMMC 2.0 guidance tied to NIST SP 800‑171, and FedRAMP Moderate expectations for CUI in cloud.
        • Contracts control: solicitations, KO direction, and agency overlays (e.g., DHS 4300A/B, FISMA/RMF) may tighten or relax obligations.
