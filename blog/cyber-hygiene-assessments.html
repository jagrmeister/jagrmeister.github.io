Introducing ABI Consulting’s Cyber Hygiene Assessment Packages

September 9, 2025 • by Damian Romano

For many small and mid-sized businesses (SMBs), cybersecurity feels like a moving target. The market is crowded with enterprise-level offerings, vague “contact us for pricing” models, and consulting engagements that can feel out of reach. What’s often missing is a clear, affordable path for SMBs to evaluate where they stand — without the overhead of a full audit or a six-figure security program.

That’s where ABI Consulting’s Cyber Hygiene Assessment packages come in.

Why We Built These Packages

We’ve seen too many SMBs left without options when it comes to understanding their security posture. Large providers focus on enterprise budgets, and even when smaller companies want to invest, they often can’t find straightforward pricing or scoped assessments designed for their size.

Our goal was to create an entry point — an assessment model that balances affordability with rigor, giving SMB leaders a way to quickly understand their risks, prioritize actions, and build confidence in front of customers, partners, and regulators.

Who These Assessments Are For

  • SMBs without dedicated security teams who need independent validation of IT practices.
  • Leaders preparing for customer or regulatory questions and seeking assurance that their basics are covered.
  • Organizations looking for a baseline before moving toward NIST CSF, ISO 27001, or SOC 2.

In short, these packages are for any SMB that wants third-party assurance without the enterprise-grade price tag.

What You Get

  • A structured assessment aligned to NIST CSF 2.0 and SMB-friendly best practices.
  • Clear, prioritized findings — no jargon.
  • Practical recommendations sized for SMB budgets and operations.
  • A concise deliverable you can share with executives, boards, or customers.

Beyond the Assessment: vCISO & Ad Hoc Support

For SMBs without in-house security staff, an assessment is just the start. ABI Consulting offers fractional vCISO services and ad hoc support to close gaps, handle customer questionnaires, or prepare for audits — experienced leadership without a full-time hire.

The Bottom Line

Cybersecurity shouldn’t be out of reach for SMBs. With ABI Consulting’s Cyber Hygiene Assessment packages, you get clarity, comfort, and an actionable plan — without hidden fees or unnecessary complexity. And if you need continued support, we’re here to step in as your trusted advisor.
